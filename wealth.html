Wealth & Financial Strategy

Genuine wealth management
is a discipline, not a product.

Preserving and growing wealth requires more than a savings plan or a standard superannuation account. It requires a clear strategy — one that accounts for your income, your assets, your obligations, your tax position and the life you intend to live.

At J.P. Bergman, we take a genuinely holistic view of your financial position. We examine everything — what you earn, what you own, what you owe, and what you want — and build a strategy that works in concert across every dimension of your financial life.

We do not sell products. We provide advice. The distinction matters — because our recommendations are made in your interest, not in the interest of a commission structure or a product shelf.

Our Approach

What wealth management
looks like with us.

Financial Planning

A comprehensive financial plan is the foundation of everything. We map your current position against your long-term goals and build a clear, actionable strategy to close the gap — reviewing it regularly as life evolves.

Superannuation Strategy

Your superannuation is likely your largest long-term asset. We review fund selection, contribution strategies, investment options and transition-to-retirement planning to ensure your super is working as hard as possible.

Retirement Planning

Retirement is not an event — it is a phase that requires careful preparation. We build income strategies, cashflow projections and drawdown plans that give you the confidence to retire on your terms, when you choose.

Tax-Effective Structuring

The structure through which you hold and manage your assets has a direct impact on your tax position. We work with your accountant or our trusted referral network to ensure your affairs are arranged as efficiently as possible.

Asset Protection

Wealth built over a lifetime can be vulnerable to events that are difficult to predict. We review your exposure and put structures in place — trusts, insurances and legal arrangements — that protect what matters most.

Estate & Succession Planning

Ensuring your wealth passes to the right people, in the right way, at the right time requires deliberate planning. We coordinate with estate planning specialists to make certain your wishes are reflected in a robust, legally sound structure.
