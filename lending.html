Bespoke Credit Solutions

When conventional lending
isn't enough.

Traditional institutions are bound by process. Their credit policies, internal timelines and risk appetite do not accommodate every legitimate opportunity — and in a market where timing is everything, that rigidity has a cost.

Private lending provides a different path — one defined by structure, speed and flexibility. We work with a select network of private lenders and non-bank credit providers to facilitate funding solutions that are tailored to the genuine merits of your position, not the constraints of a bank's policy manual.

Our clients include property developers requiring construction finance, investors bridging between transactions, business owners accessing working capital and high-net-worth individuals with complex or non-standard borrowing requirements.

Lending Solutions

What we arrange.

Bridging Finance

Short-term funding that bridges the gap between buying and selling, or between development stages. Fast approval, flexible terms, structured around your timeline.

Development Finance

Construction and development funding for residential, mixed-use and commercial projects. We work with lenders who understand feasibility and project risk — not just serviceability.

Caveat & Second Mortgage

Fast-access funding secured against existing property equity. Suitable for urgent capital requirements where conventional approval timelines are not viable.

Commercial Lending

Business finance, commercial property acquisition and working capital solutions structured around the operational realities of your business — not a standardised product.

Non-Conforming Loans

For borrowers who do not fit traditional lending criteria — self-employed individuals, those with complex income structures, or recent credit history issues — we access specialist lenders who assess the full picture.

High-Net-Worth Structuring

Complex borrowing arrangements for clients with significant assets, multiple entities or sophisticated investment structures. Discretion and precision are paramount.
