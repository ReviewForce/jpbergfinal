Our Practice

Finance practised
as it ought to be.

J.P. Bergman was founded on a straightforward conviction: that every individual and family deserves access to the same quality of financial advice once reserved for institutions and the privileged few.

Based in Melbourne, we are a privately held practice with no institutional allegiances and no product obligations. Our advice is independent, our fees are transparent, and our focus is singular — the long-term financial wellbeing of our clients.

Over more than fifteen years in practice, we have served clients across Victoria and throughout Australia — from first home buyers and young professionals building their financial foundations to established investors managing significant and complex wealth.

What has not changed across that time is our approach: listen carefully, advise honestly, execute with precision, and remain with our clients through every stage of their financial journey.

Our Values

The principles that define
our practice.

Independence

We carry no institutional allegiances and no product quotas. Our recommendations are driven entirely by what is right for the client — selected from the full breadth of the market, not a preferred panel curated for our own benefit. You will never receive advice shaped by a commission structure you are unaware of.

Integrity

We say what we mean, and we mean what we say. Our fees are disclosed, our conflicts are declared, and our advice is given with the same candour we would apply to our own affairs. That is the only basis on which a long-term relationship of genuine value can be built.

Intelligence

Markets move, legislation changes and lender appetites shift. We invest continuously in our knowledge of current conditions — maintaining active relationships with lenders, institutions and specialists — so that our clients always benefit from advice that is current, accurate and grounded in genuine expertise.

Melbourne, Victoria

Rooted in Melbourne.
Trusted across Australia.

We have been part of the Melbourne financial community for over fifteen years. That history and those relationships are a genuine advantage for our clients.

Why J.P. Bergman

What sets us apart.

01

We are not a transactional practice

We form long-term relationships with our clients. Many have been with us for a decade or more. That continuity means we understand their history, their goals and their preferences — and our advice reflects that depth of understanding.

02

Full service, fully coordinated

Because we work across lending, wealth and investment, we see the whole picture. Decisions in one area of your financial life affect all others — and we account for that in the advice we give.

03

Direct access to senior expertise

You will work directly with experienced advisers — not with a junior team member who escalates to someone you rarely see. The person you meet is the person managing your affairs.

04

Transparent fees, no hidden costs

We disclose exactly what we charge, how we are compensated, and what you can expect in return. There are no surprises — only a clear understanding of the value we provide.
